Qibla iFinder Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Qibla iFinder collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users (each, a "User") of the Qibla iFinder mobile application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

Qibla iFinder uses the Users location while the mobile application is in use. Qibla iFinder does not store or retain any data from user.

3. How We Use Collected Information

Data such as the Users location is used to calculate the bearing angle to accurately point the user to the direction of the Kaaba.

4. Sharing Your Personal Information

Qibla iFinder does not share any information to third-parties or other.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy is not subject to change.
